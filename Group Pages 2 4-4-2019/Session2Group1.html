Task:

Add accurate and complete information about the term "leaf blade" to the CAREX Ontology.

This is what you know about the term"leaf blade". Take a minute to become familiar with the information.
  • "Leaf blade" is not in the CAREX ontology
  • Definition: Leaf blade is the expanded, often flattened portion of the leaf
  • All leaf blades are part of some leaves, and “leaf” is in the CAREX Ontology (material anatomical entity > leaf)
  • All leaf blades have a leaf apex, which is the outer end of a leaf. “lLeaf apex” is not in the CAREX Ontology
  • "Leaf-blade" exists in the CAREX Ontology (material anatomical entity > blade > leaf-blade) and it means the same as "leaf blade"
  • Leaf blade has been used in a published sentence: "leaves : widest leaf_blades 2 – 4mm wide"
  • Sentence describing a taxon: Carex spicata
