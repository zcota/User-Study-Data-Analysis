 <h5 class="card-title">What you know about <b>"leaf blade":</h5>
 

<ul class="lead">
  <li><b><i>Definition:</i> </b>Leaf blade is the expanded, often flattened portion of  the leaf</li>
  <li><b><i>Part of:</i></b> Add "leaf" is part of - All leaf blades are part of some leaves, and “leaf” is in the CAREX Ontology (material anatomical entity > leaf)</li>
  <li><b><i>Has part:</i></b> Add "leaf apex" as has part - All leaf blades have a leaf apex, which is the outer end of a leaf. “leaf apex” is not in the CAREX Ontology</li>
  <li><b><i>Synonym:</i></b> Add "leaf-blade" as synonym - "Leaf-blade" is already in the CAREX Ontology (material anatomical entity > blade > leaf-blade)  and it means the same as "leaf blade" </li>
  <li><b><i>Sentence using the term:</i></b> "leaves : widest leaf_blades 2 – 4mm wide"</li>
  <li><b><i>Sentence describing a taxon:</i> </b>Carex spicata </li>
</ul>
